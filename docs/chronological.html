The Chronological System

Overview

The Chronological System is a simple, date-based filing system. It's the perfect way to organize files where the date is the most important piece of information, such as photos, videos, journals, or logs.

How it Works

The system is based on a simple hierarchy of folders:

  • A main folder for the year (e.g., "2024")
  • Subfolders for each month (e.g., "01_January", "02_February")

You can then place your files directly into the month folders, or create further subdirectories for specific days or events.
